Fellah Koftesi Receipe

  • for 6 person people
  • 1h prep time
  • 30 min cooking time

1st step:Transfer 2 cups of fine bulgur into a bowl and add 2.5 cups of hot water to it.

2nd step : Cover the top and wait until the bulgur swells.

3rd step :Add 1 egg and 1 tablespoon of pepper paste into the swelling bulgur. Add 1 cup of flour little by little and start kneading.

4th step :Add 1 teaspoon of salt and pepper and knead well until combined.

5th step:Wet your hands slightly. Take a little bit larger than a hazelnut from the dough and roll them in the palms of your hands. After rolling, make a hole by pressing the middle part with your finger.

6nd step :Transfer the meatballs you have shaped into a lightly floured bowl and mix well.

7th step :Throw your meatballs into the boiling water in the pot and boil them for 10-15 minutes until they are soft.

8th step :Strain the boiled meatballs with the help of a colander and put them in my pyrex

9th step:Take 1.5 tea glasses of olive oil and 1 tablespoon of pepper paste in a pan. Add 1 cup of grated and crushed tomatoes on it.

10th step:When the consistency becomes slightly dense, add 2 cloves of grated garlic, 1 teaspoon of chili pepper, 1 teaspoon of black pepper and 1 teaspoon of salt and mix.

11th step:Then pour the sauce you prepared on it and mix it well.

12th step :Transfer to a serving plate and serve with finely chopped parsley. Good luck.
